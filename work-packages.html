Work Packages

Fin4Good is organised into six Work Packages (WPs). Together they build a pathway from learning to practical application—curriculum, piloting, tools, collaboration, and dissemination.

WP1

Project Management

Ensures project coordination, governance, quality assurance, and timely delivery across all partners and outputs.

  • Project planning, coordination, and partner alignment
  • Quality management and risk monitoring
  • Reporting and overall project oversight
WP2

Development of Educational Curriculum

Designs and develops the curriculum that forms the learning pathway on microfinance and the social economy.

  • Curriculum design and learning outcomes definition
  • Content development (modules, materials, resources)
  • Alignment across partners for consistency and quality
WP3

Curriculum Piloting and Feedback

Tests the curriculum through pilots, gathers feedback from participants, and improves the pathway for scale.

  • Pilot sessions and learner testing
  • Feedback collection and analysis
  • Iteration and refinement of curriculum content
WP4

Gamification Tool for Microfinance and Social Economy

Develops an interactive gamification tool to support learning and practical understanding through engagement.

  • Design of game mechanics aligned with learning objectives
  • Tool development and testing
  • Integration with the learning pathway and dissemination-ready formats
WP5

Collaboration and Networking

Builds relationships and knowledge exchange among partners and stakeholders across Europe to strengthen impact.

  • Stakeholder engagement and partnership building
  • Collaboration activities across countries and institutions
  • Networking initiatives to support long-term adoption
WP6

Communication and Dissemination

Ensures project outputs reach the right audiences through communication, dissemination and visibility actions.

  • Project communications (updates, events, news)
  • Dissemination of curriculum, tools and results
  • Visibility strategy to support uptake beyond the project
