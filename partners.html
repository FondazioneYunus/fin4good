People & Partners

Fin4Good is delivered by a consortium of universities and expert organisations. Each partner leads specific work packages and contributes to shared outputs.

University of Bologna (UNIBO)
Coordinator
  • Overall project coordination and governance.
  • Leads the definition of the course structure and the development of curriculum content.
Fondazione Yunus Italia ETS (FYI)
Communication & dissemination
  • Leads communication and dissemination activities.
  • Supports outreach materials and website development.
University of Sarajevo (UNSA)
Network of Networks & Virtual Summer Program
  • Leads the “Network of Networks” to connect universities and external organisations.
  • Coordinates local partnerships and the Virtual Summer Program.
University “Aleksandër Xhuvani” of Elbasan (UNIEL)
Field research & curriculum piloting
  • Leads field research and stakeholder consultation.
  • Leads the pilot implementation and feedback analysis for curriculum improvement.
University of Agder (UiA)
Gamification platform & serious game
  • Leads the design of the gamification platform.
  • Leads the serious game development and interactive tutorials.
Academy of Entrepreneurship (AKEP)
Practice & social entrepreneurship
  • Contributes practical expertise from microfinance and social entrepreneurship ecosystems.
  • Supports content design with an applied, target-group perspective.
